HOW TO PLAY

Master the loop. Survive longer. Eliminate faster.

OBJECTIVE

Survive by fighting hard and taking down all enemies to win.

CONTROLS

W A S D — Move

Mouse — Aim

Left Click — Shoot

GAMEPLAY LOOP

1 DROP IN

Spawn into the area.

2 PREPARE

FLook for the enemies.

3 ELIMINATE

Fight enemies and kill them all.

4 REPEAT

If eliminated, restart.

TIPS

  • Stay a little farther while eliminating enemies.
  • Kill enemies one at a time.
  • Hide between walls while shooting enemies, so you won't die.
